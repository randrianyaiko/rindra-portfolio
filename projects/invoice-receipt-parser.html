Invoice & Receipt Parser

This project leverages OCR technology with Tesseract alongside Google Gemini large language models to extract structured JSON data from unstructured invoices and receipts. By combining precise optical character recognition and advanced language understanding, it automates document processing, reducing manual data entry and enhancing business efficiency.

Technology used:

    LangChain
    Gemini AI
    Streamlit
