Invoice & Receipt Parser

This project uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology with Tesseract and Google Gemini LLMs to extract structured JSON data from unstructured invoices and receipts. It streamlines financial document processing by accurately capturing key information such as totals, dates, and vendor details.

Business Value

Businesses can automate tedious data entry tasks, reduce errors in financial records, and accelerate invoice processing workflows.
